Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to provide marriage and relationship counseling services to couples and family members who want to resolve conflicts and/or grow closer together. This site is for our prospective and current clients to learn about their options and schedule an appointment with the therapist of their choice.

Audience

We connect individuals with trained therapists to enable them to take accountablity for themselves and learn / workshop how they can establish boundaries and live within the bounds they set for themselves and others. Additionaly, those who wish participate in group and couples therapy can find a space to work together to improve their dynamics through open communication and counselor mediation.

Home page

WHAT WE DO

Moore to Love is a place for people to find a way to connect more deeply with their partner. Whether you have been married for sixty years or dating for a few months, everyone needs the opportunity to work together on themselves and their relationships. Learn about your options, get to know our therapists, and book a life-changing appointment today.

OUR THERAPISTS

Moore to Love has three main therapists who specialise in different areas of treatment. Each one has years of experience helping couples just like you. First, we have Audrey Moore is our founder. She is EMDR certified and works primarily with married couples. Our second therapist is Shelly Morrison. Working primarily with younger families, she uses ART therapy to help repair relationships that need a special touch. Our final therapist is Jason Conchake. With thirty years in the industry and a specialization in CBT therapy, he is always a great option for those who want to stretch out of their comfort zones to reach new heights. No matter your choice, our therapists know exactly how to guide you to become your best self!

HOW CAN YOU CONTACT US?

>

Just fill out the box below with your name, number, and your preferred meeting times, and we’ll reach out to schedule your first appointment. Scheduling is completely hassle-free. Once your appointment is set, make sure to arrive 15 minutes early before your first appointment to fill out the appropriate paperwork, and your therapist will be right with you. We look forward to joining you on your self-improvement journey.

Special Information

>

To schedule an appointment with one of our therapists, all you need is your contact information, including the names of you and your partner, which therapist you’d like to meet with, and a preferred meeting time. Then, you can use our “contact us” page to get in touch with our office to schedule that first meeting with your \ therapist.
